IESL RoboGames 2014 University Category

IESL RoboGames, University Category Competition 2014 has been organized with the main objective of giving an opportunity to university students from all over the country to experience the only national level robot competition in Sri Lanka. This is a rare opportunity for university participants expecting to pursue higher studies in Universities in the field of Robotics.

By organizing a competition of this genre and this magnitude, The Robotics Club of Department of Computer Science and Engineering , University of Moratuwa expect to improve awareness and positive attitudes in university students about Robotics and connected fields in general and the Inter-relationship among university populace will be enhanced connected to the said fields.

The Robotics Club of Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Moratuwa, invites all undergraduates to reap maximum benefit out of these opportunities and to get a head start in their future careers and fields of studies.

General Guidelines

  • Participants are advised to form a team of up to 5 undergraduates. Any number of teams from a university can enroll in the competition.

  • All the team members should be students of same university at the time of their participation in the competition.

  • Each team should provide valid identification document of the university on the competition day to prove the eligibility to participate in the competition.

Game Task

Game task is designed based on a warehouse theme. Objective is to build an autonomous robot that can follow white lines and deliver payloads to a specific location.
