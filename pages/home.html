IESL RoboGames 2014

The IT & Communication Engineering Sectional Committee of the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL) in collaboration with Department of Computer Science and Engineering of University of Moratuwa proudly presents "IESL RoboGames 2014", challenging the youth of Sri Lanka to use their creativity to construct and program autonomous robots.

RoboGames is a call to all the enthusiasts out there, it may be the pros eager to show off their colours, or the beginners waiting for a chance to enter this delightful world of automation. Department of Computer Science and Engineering of University of Moratuwa is always willing to extend a helping hand, to answer your questions and to solve your doubts. It’s time to rack your brains and come up with an innovative design that deserves the title ‘Winner of RoboGames 2014’

The competition will be held at the Annual Engineering Exhibition – Techno at BMICH and awards will be presented at the conclusion of the Techno 2014 Exhibition.
