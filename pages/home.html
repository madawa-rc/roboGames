IESL Robo Games 2014

Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL) in collaboration with Department of Computer Science and Engineering of University of Moratuwa proudly presents "IESL RoboGames 2014", challenging the youth of Sri Lanka to use their creativity to construct and program autonomous robots.

The competition will be held in two categories; School Category and Undergraduate Category.
