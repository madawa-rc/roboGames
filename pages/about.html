About IESL RoboGames

The IESL, in its effort to promote and recognize the talents of next generation of professionals in the country, is organizing a competition based event targeting school children and undergraduates. This annual event called “RoboGames” is organized through the information Technology and communications Engineering Sectional Committee of the IESL in collaboration with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of Moratuwa.

The competition will be held at the Annual Engineering Exhibition – Techno at BMICH and awards will be presented at the conclusion of the Techno 2014 Exhibition.
